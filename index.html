About Us

Our core values and commitment to the community

Welcome to Valrise Gaming.

We are a gaming community with a few core values that we wish to pursue. Our goal is to create an unparalleled gaming experience for all our members.

Quality & Customization

The first and foremost, we wish to provide quality servers that were built for our community by our developers. We need fully customized scripts that can fit our vision and be adjusted to the needs of the players depending on their feedback and submitted suggestions.

Positive Environment

Moving on to our next value, which is positive and friendly environment within our community. We will not harbor hostile and negative atmosphere. Everybody must be respected.

Community Driven

We also agree with the fact that the players create a community. Those members and their feedback must be respected and taken into consideration with development of this community. We will appreciate any feedback you may provide us with.

Approachable Staff

Furthermore, we wish to ensure that our staff team will be fully approachable by you, the players. Approachable and friendly staff is what we pursue to have, a staff team that serves the players, and perform their jobs for the good of the server, not for any personal interests.

In conclusion, our community wishes to ensure that all our players will be playing in a positive environment with a friendly community, in a server with quality scripts and approachable staff team. We may expand to other games in future... slowly and steadily in order to ensure that we get everything created properly and so that the players can enjoy.
