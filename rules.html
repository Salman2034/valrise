Community Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, please read and follow our server rules.

  1. Accounts:
    • - No sharing, multi accounts, VPN/proxies.
    • - No celeb/offensive/impersonating names; use proper capitalization.
  2. Chat:
    • - No spam, caps, ads; English only in public.
  3. Behavior:
    • - Be respectful/mature, RP disrespect only IC, no disruption.
    • - No flirting, e-dating, baiting for sexual attention, sexual roleplay, or inappropriate messages and requests on such basis.
  4. Bugs/Mods:

  5. Common Sense:
    • - No unfair advantage, discomfort, or aiding rulebreakers.
  6. Admins:
    • - Respect staff decisions; donÃ¢â‚¬â„¢t quit/ignore to evade.
  7. Trade:
    • - No selling/trading accounts/items for real money.
  8. Reports:
    • - Only real rulebreaks; no fake reports/proof.
  1. Powergaming:
    • - Do not force actions upon other players without giving them a chance to respond.
    • - Do not roleplay the use of items you don't possess through scripts.
    • - Avoid roleplaying actions that are physically impossible for a human, like killing with one punch.
  2. Metagaming:
    • - Do not use out-of-character information in-character.
    • - Do not share in-character information in out-of-character chats.
    • - Do not act on information obtained through out-of-character means.
  3. Failure to Roleplay, Poor Roleplay, Unrealistic Roleplay:
    • - Roleplay is mandatory; refusing for any reason is not allowed.
    • - Only administrators can pause an active situation.
    • - Avoid inappropriate roleplay (sexual, gore, etc.) without consent.
    • - Roleplay /seat, /drag, /(un)cuff, /(un)tie actions
    • - Stay in-character; use appropriate in-character chats.
    • - Suiciding to avoid a situation or consequences prohibited without admin permission
  4. Fear Roleplay:
    • - Roleplay fear when outnumbered (three or more opponents if alone, five or more if two).
    • - Not mandatory when both sides have larger numbers (over three players on each side).
  5. Character Kill:
    • - Group leaders can character kill members as long as sensible reason exists.
    • - Players committing character kill to avoid court consequences require management approval.
    • - Once character killed, your name must be changed.
  1. Deathmatch, Killing, and Dying:
    • - Do not shoot or kill players without a valid in-character reason.
    • - Do not start shootouts over petty reasons (insults, traffic stops, accidents, etc.).
    • - In petty police situations (traffic stops, checkpoints):
    • - Do not fire unless evasion is impossible (flipped, disabled, boxed in).
    • - Do not call backup; only vehicle occupants may assist.
    • - Do not kill with a knife without prior roleplay or backstab during combat.
    • - No revenge killing; once dead in a scene, you cannot hunt those involved.
    • - Do not return to a scene after death; wait for it to conclude.
    • - Do not request backup for minor situations (traffic stops, checkpoints, etc.).
    • - Do not kill a friend or ally to prevent seizure or robbery.
    • - Do not kill EMS without warning and 10 seconds to comply.
  2. Robberies and Kidnaps:

    Robberies

    • - No robbing police lockers or group HQs without management approval.
    • - No robbing walkie-talkies or phones unless in a kidnapping scene.
    • - No stickup robberies ("STOP, HANDS UP!") except at active drug locations.
    • - No robbing random knocked-out players on the street.
    • - No robbing new players (under level 5).

    Kidnaps

    • - Not allowed in safezones (including group safezones).
    • - No kidnapping new players (under level 5).
    • - Rival group members can be kidnapped without admin permission.
    • - Civilians and on-duty government require senior admin approval.
    • - All kidnaps must involve roleplay and allow negotiations.
  3. Safezones:
    • - All government buildings (PDs, hospitals, fire stations, city halls, etc.).
    • - Group HQs with marked zones on the map.
    • - Casinos.
  4. Aiding:
    • - Players may aid each other if:
    • - They are in the same scripted group or squad.
    • - They are in different groups but in the same scripted alliance.
    • - Non-criminals may aid police when directly asked in a scene.
  1. Gambling:
    • - Gambling scams are not allowed inside casinos.
    • - Use scripted methods to prevent being scammed.
    • - No refund if scam occurs and it was preventable.
  2. Property Scams:
    • - Property scams are not allowed.
    • - Victim will be refunded if scam occurs.
  3. Vehicle Scams:
    • - Vehicle scams are not allowed.
    • - Victim will be refunded if scam occurs.
  4. Illegal Scams:
    • - It is allowed to scam in illegal deals (drugs, weapons, ...).
    • - No refunds unless the scammer broke a rule (ie. quitting in deal).
  1. Vehicles:
    • - Do not carjack players unless they're actively being pursued.
    • - No standing on moving vehicles (carsurfing), except on specific ones.
    • - Allowed vehicles for carsurfing include dumper, bobcat, patriot, flatbed, walton, etc.
    • - Exception for kidnapping victims in the trunk.
    • - Avoid intentionally reckless driving causing collisions.
    • - No parking vehicles on top of other players (carkill).
    • - Limit on-foot rams during shootouts.
    • - No using helicopters or airplanes to ram on-foot players or vehicles without admin permission for events.
  1. Safe:
    • - Safe must not be placed on ceilings (roof) or underneath the floor.
    • - That means, safe may only be placed inside horizontal walls.
    • - Safe must not be blocked by solid, immovable furniture objects.
    • - That means, as long as the safe can be accessed by anyone, it is fine.
    • - Safe can be hidden inside the wall (can be invisible to the eye) as long as it's accessible by command.
    • - Safe must not be placed far away from the wall to make others unable to access it.
  2. Furniture:
    • - Do not build solid, tall walls around your home using furniture.
    • - Max that's allowed is making a low hedge around the property, if it looks appropriate.
    • - Do not use solid, immovable furniture to block access to weed pots or weed growing area.
    • - Do not use solid, immovable furniture to block access to storage safe.
    • - Do not use solid immovable furniture to obstruct or make inaccessible drug production areas (heroin shacks, journey vans, cocaine labs, ...)
  3. Furniture can be used to create normal furniture you can get in everyday life, create a small hedge around the property, or use walls to place over existing walls as a way to re-texturize them.
  4. Furniture CANNOT be used to build second floors, tall fences/walls around the property, build platforms, build structures, or build anything in general.
  1. Law Enforcement (and Military):
    • - Law enforcement can open fire on vehicles aiding a pursuit if they interfere.
    • - No intentionally suspecting players randomly for annoyance.
    • - No suspecting players with intent to increase their wanted time.
    • - Special vehicles "Hunter," "Hydra," "Seasparrow" can only engage fleeing aircraft, not ground targets.
    • - "Rhino" and "AC130" use requires management permission.
    • - No annoying or disruptive behavior without reasonable purpose.
    • - Corrupt activities need prior management permission with in-character justification.
    • - Petty corrupt activities that don't cause major financial loss don't need permission.
    • - Law enforcement can shoot suspect vehicles if rammed on-foot, only at the incident location.
    • - Drive-by shooting allowed on suspect vehicle if:
      • - Regardless of the vehicle, the suspect has at any point opened fire at law enforcement.
      • - Regardless of the crime, the suspect is using duneride or monster truck.
  1. Generally:
    • - All jobs must be utilized for their intended purpose, in the intended way.
    • - Any abuse, misuse of a job may result in punishment.
  2. Firefighter:
    • - Firefighters cannot use fire extinguisher, chainsaw, or water cannon against players.
  3. 3. EMS:
    • - Medics cannot participate in turf wars.
    • - Players cannot join EMS duty solely to revive friends or allies.
  4. Mechanic:
    • - Players cannot join Mechanic duty solely to repair friends' or allies' vehicles.
  1. Jewelry, Museum, Bank Heist:
    • - Hostage advised but not required.
    • - Negotiator check allowed for hostage status.
    • - Police may breach if no legitimate hostage.
    • - No harm to compliant hostages.
    • - Hostage must be released after the heist.
    • - No aiders outside/heist members.
    • - No tactical forces or allied groups.
    • - Drive-by allowed for both parties.
  2. SecuriCar Heist:
    • - No shooting the securicar heist members until they are transporting the securicar.
    • - Securicar heist members are allowed to shoot anyone attempting to steal the truck from them.
    • - Everyone is allowed to shoot the heist members, if intending to deliver the truck themselves.
  3. Other Missions:
    • - Police are allowed in the following missions (without SWAT):
    • - Jewelry, Museum, Bank, Securicar Heist
    • - Smuggle Mission (the regular briefcase smuggle mission)
    • - Police are not allowed to participate in any other missions unless specified above.
    • - Do note, police do not get alerts of missions ongoing, so situation might occur where they engage you by mistake.
  1. Turf Rules:
    • - Only turf defenders and allies can attack turf attackers.
    • - Job abilities and vehicles are prohibited in turfs.
    • - Police cannot intervene until the turf stops flashing.
    • - Explosives allowed, except:
      • - IEDs attached to aircraft.
      • - IEDs planted/detonated within 1 minute of exiting an aircraft.
  2. Attacks:
    • - No attacks near group HQs without admin approval.
    • - Hostile actions include shooting, kidnapping, robbing, etc.
  3. Business & Criminal Group Rules:
    • - Passive groups have no enemies/allies and cannot:
      • - Engage in turf wars.
      • - Attack other group members.
      • - Join alliances.
    • - They may defend drugs (weed field, heroin shack, journey van).
    • - Business groups must use vehicles appropriate to their specialization:
      • - Furnishing: Vans, pickup trucks.
      • - Trucking: Trucks, vans.
      • - Taxi: Must have a taxi sign.
      • - Rental/Dealership: May have sports vehicles for selling/renting.
    • - No storing illegal items in business group vehicles/properties.
    • - No management permissions for ranks below level 95.
    • - Group leaders must have VAC working.
  4. Government Group Rules:
    • - Leaders (Commissioner, Director, General, Chief, etc.) must be approved by Server Management before appointment.
    • - Must comply with server standards and rules, including group lore (vehicles, skins, weapons, ranks, etc.).
    • - Server Management may revoke changes/decrees violating Rule 9.4.2.
    • - Server Management may remove leaders deemed unfit.
    • - Server Management acts as the "United States Congress" and "Office of the United States President" in-character.
    • - Government factions cannot operate outside their jurisdiction without an invitation from the governing agency.
  1. Characters Rules:
    • - You cannot in any way, shape or form, directly or indirectly, interact with your own characters.
    • - You cannot transfer money or assets between your own characters.
    • - You cannot use third persons to transfer assets or interact with your own characters.
    • - You cannot use information you obtain with one character in your other character's gameplay.
    • - It is up to group leaders to decide whether they will accept individuals who use characters or not.
    • - Each character must be in different groups, you cannot be in same group through several characters.
    • - Each character must have a unique name, you cannot have similar names between characters.
